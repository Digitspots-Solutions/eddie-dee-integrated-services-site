About Us

Eddie Dee Integrated Services is a leading manufacturing company based in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. We are committed to delivering high-quality manufactured products that meet international standards while serving the Nigerian market with excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern manufacturing technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce products that our clients can depend on. Located in the heart of Uyo's Federal Housing Estate, we pride ourselves on precision engineering, quality control, and timely delivery.

Our integrated approach ensures that every stage of the manufacturing process maintains the highest standards, from raw material sourcing to final product delivery.
